##  **DESIGN PRINCIPLES:**

### **1. Speed:**
- Homepage loads in < 2 seconds
- Search results instant (< 100ms)
- Payment form pre-fills where possible
- One-tap actions (repeat payment)

### **2. Clarity:**
- Clear step-by-step process (Step 1 of 3)
- No jargon (use simple language)
- Visual progress indicators
- Confirmation at every step

### **3. Trust:**
- Official AMAC branding everywhere
- Security badges visible
- Clear contact information
- Real-time status updates

### **4. Accessibility:**
- Works on all devices (phone, tablet, desktop)
- Works on slow internet (progressive loading)
- Works offline (PWA features)
- Large buttons (easy to tap)
- High contrast colors

### **5. Delight:**
- Smooth animations
- Success celebrations (confetti on payment success)
- Friendly language ("You're all set!" not "Transaction complete")
- Helpful tips throughout
